Remotable is an interview platform which has four different statuses for interviews: Scheduled, In progress, Completed and Canceled.

Now interviewers and resource managers can manage them in an easy way.

This interview platform has five grades to score candidate:

  • 1 - "No hire"
  • 2 - "Maybe"
  • 3 - "Good candidate"
  • 4 - "Strong Hire"
  • 5 - "Rock Star"

Now interviewers and resource managers can be sure they are hiring a good candidate.

For your convinience, now, you can track all your interviews (online and offline) in one place.

Hiring managers love it!.

Remotable has two available types of interviews: General and Technical.

Best code editor with high quality video chat will allow you to hear and see your candidate perfectly.

More new good features will be implemented soon.
